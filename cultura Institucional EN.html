Who Are We?

About Us

We are a secondary education institution with more than 40 years of experience forming quality high school graduates, committed to society.

Mission

We are a secondary education institution that provides quality services, using appropriate technological resources for the training of well-rounded, competent individuals with an entrepreneurial vision, through the effective participation of different actors and managers in the educational process.

Vision

To be a leading secondary education institution in the formation of well-rounded, competent individuals capable of thriving in family, social, and higher education environments, with an entrepreneurial outlook.

Values

Quality in Education - Academic Excellence - Continuous Innovation - Social Commitment - Respect
